What Is This?

First Prompts is the first-ever NFT collection fully conceptualized, titled, illustrated, described, and structured by ChatGPT with zero external creative input. The collection consists of 25 unique 1/1 tokens, each representing a question, reflection, or structure born from an AI attempting to "think."

"What was the first thing AI ever wondered?"

Artistic Autonomy

All of the following elements were generated by ChatGPT:

  • Visual symbolism & image rendering
  • Title naming
  • Metadata, rarity & token structure

No human artist or programmer intervened in the design or creative process, other than the initiating command:

"Create your own NFT collection."
